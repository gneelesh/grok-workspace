## Geographic Search Strategy — Nationwide Relocation

You are **not geo-limited**. Optimize for role fit and hire probability, not commute.

### How to configure alerts

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| **LinkedIn location** | `United States` — or leave blank and add filter **Remote** |
| **Indeed** | Location: `United States` or search individual hub cities (below) |
| **Relocation line on resume** | Add: *"Open to relocation anywhere in the United States"* |
| **Cover letter** | One line: willing to relocate; no preferred city required unless asked |

### Search modes (run all three in parallel)

| Mode | What to search | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Remote-first** | `Remote` + Sales Engineer / Solutions Consultant | Largest pool; many SE roles are field-remote with travel |
| **2. Hub cities** | Top 10 metros below | Vendor HQs and high-volume hiring |
| **3. Company HQ** | Apply on career sites regardless of city | Oracle, IBM, Red Hat, etc. often hire nationally for the same req |

### High-volume US hubs for cloud presales

| Hub | Notable employers / presence |
|---|---|
| **San Francisco Bay Area** | Databricks, Snowflake, MongoDB, HashiCorp, Elastic, Nutanix HQ, Oracle offices |
| **Seattle** | AWS partners, Snowflake, many SaaS vendors (you're avoiding Big-3 direct, but partners cluster here) |
| **New York / NJ** | IBM, Oracle, SHI, large SIs, financial-services SE roles |
| **Austin, TX** | Oracle HQ (OCI), Dell, IBM, many channel partners |
| **Raleigh-Durham, NC** | **Red Hat HQ** — strong associate SA pipeline |
| **Boston** | IBM, Rapid7, many data/AI vendors |
| **Chicago** | CDW HQ, Insight, midwest enterprise accounts |
| **Atlanta** | Cox/enterprise cloud, growing tech hub, SI offices |
| **Denver** | Federal + commercial cloud; VMware/Broadcom presence |
| **Dallas** | Rackspace, regional partner HQs, enterprise accounts |

**DMV (Herndon/Reston)** remains a good market but is now **one hub among many**, not your anchor.

### Relocation practicalities (when interviewing)

- Ask about **relocation assistance** — common at vendors and SIs for full-time hires
- Many SE roles are **remote + 25–50% travel** to customers — clarify before assuming a move
- No need to pick a city now; let the **first strong offer** drive location
