## Master Boolean Strings

### Alert 1: Remote Cloud SE

```
("Sales Engineer" OR "Solutions Engineer" OR "Presales Engineer" OR "Pre-Sales Engineer" OR "Solutions Consultant")
AND (cloud OR kubernetes OR k8s OR "cloud native" OR container OR devops)
NOT ("Senior Director" OR "Principal" OR "Staff" OR "VP" OR "Director of")
```

## Negative Keywords (skip these postings)

Manually skip or add `NOT` in Boolean:

| Skip if title contains | Why |
|---|---|
| Principal · Distinguished · Fellow | 8–15+ YOE bar |
| Director · VP · Head of | Management, not IC presales |
| Senior Staff (without Associate) | Usually 7+ YOE |
| BDR · SDR · Account Executive | Pure sales quota unless you want pivot |
| Customer Success Manager (no "Engineer") | Post-sales, not presales |
| Support Engineer · IT Support | Not solutions selling |
